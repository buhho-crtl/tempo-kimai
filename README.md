# Tempo – Time Tracker for Kimai

**Native iOS and macOS app for Kimai time tracking.**

Track your work hours, manage timesheets, and view reports — all connected directly to your self-hosted Kimai server.

[![Download on App Store](https://img.shields.io/badge/App_Store-Download-black?style=for-the-badge&logo=apple)](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6761081522)

---

## Features

- **Instant Timer** — Start and stop timers with a single tap. Active timer always visible in the iOS timer bar and macOS menu bar.
- **Timesheets** — Browse, filter, and edit your complete timesheet history by customer, project, or activity.
- **Reports** — Clear overview of tracked time by project and client with advanced date filters.
- **Menu Bar on Mac** — Tempo lives in your macOS menu bar. Current timer and daily total always one click away.
- **Native Design** — Built with SwiftUI. Feels right at home on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
- **Your Data, Your Server** — Connects directly to your Kimai instance. No middlemen, no data stored by us.

---

## Requirements

Requires an active account on a self-hosted **Kimai server (v2 or higher)**.

## Privacy Policy

Tempo does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All timesheet data and credentials are stored exclusively on your own Kimai server and on your device.

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob. Upgrading to **Tempo Pro** removes all ads and unlocks the full Reports section.
