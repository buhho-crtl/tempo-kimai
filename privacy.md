# Privacy Policy – Tempo

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## Overview

Tempo is a client app for Kimai, a self-hosted time tracking server. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Do Not Collect

Tempo does not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers. All your timesheet data, credentials, and project information are stored exclusively on your own Kimai server and on your device.

## Kimai Server Connection

Tempo communicates directly with your self-hosted Kimai instance using your server URL and API token. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage and is never sent to us.

## Advertising

The free version of Tempo displays ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information and identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can learn more at [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Upgrading to Tempo Pro removes all ads.

## Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (such as app crashes and feature usage) to improve the app. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

## In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at **support@buhhoapps.dev**
